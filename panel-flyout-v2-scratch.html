Experiment paused
This one didn't work out the way we hoped.

This prototype explored an interaction where selecting a card would slide the stack off to the right, revealing a full control panel from underneath — creating the impression that the panel was physically tucked behind the stack.

The concept was promising, but the animation and layering behavior proved too difficult to get right. After several attempts, we weren't able to nail it.

The idea may be worth revisiting in a future iteration with a different approach.

Note: The slide animation runs at 2 seconds — far slower than a real product. That was intentional. Slowing it down made it easier to watch exactly what was happening at each stage, catch layering problems, and focus on the details while iterating.
