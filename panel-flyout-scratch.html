Sandbox experiment

A prototype of the card editor with a side panel instead of an accordion.

  • current model: click card → controls expand inline → pushes everything down (accordion)
  • problem: stack keeps jumping around as you open/close things, makes it hard to scan what's there
  • idea: what if selecting a card opened controls in a panel to the left instead? stack never moves
  • this is a scratch prototype of that — click any card, panel slides in from the left
  • switch between cards — panel updates but the stack stays put
  • open question: does this feel better or just different?
